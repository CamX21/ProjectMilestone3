About Spider Man

The Origin Story

Spider Man was first introduced to the world in 1962 by Stan Lee and Steve Ditko. It was about a story about a high school student named Peter Parker, who gained spider like abilities after being bitten by a spider.

Powers & Abilities

Spider Mans powers include super strength, agility, the ability to stick to walls, and his spider sense that warns him of danger. He is also known for his web shooting abilities, which allow him to swing through the city .

Movies
